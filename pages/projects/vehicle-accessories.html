Design for Vehicle Accessories: AIWAYS U5 Electric Vehicle Accessories

AIWAYS U5 內裝品牌配件延續著車款的移動家居理念，推出四款主題設計，包括裝飾盒與桌板，從妝點到辦公場景皆涵蓋。在實現場景多樣性的同時，也融入汽車內飾，使設計與空間維持連貫視覺。

The AIWAYS U5 interior brand accessories continue the vehicle’s “mobile home” concept, offering four themed designs, including decorative boxes and tabletops, covering functions from personal styling to work scenarios. While enabling diverse usage possibilities, the accessories are seamlessly integrated into the car interior, maintaining a coherent visual language throughout the space.

Continuity in Perspective/

延續 U5 車款的極簡智美理念，以明暢舒適的空間為基底，專注於流暢的機構與材質觸感，並在有限空間中維持視覺的一體性，實現車款與用戶的流暢互動。

Continuing the minimalist and intelligent aesthetic of the U5, the design builds on a bright and comfortable spatial foundation, focusing on smooth mechanisms and tactile material experiences, while maintaining visual coherence within a limited space to enable seamless interaction between the vehicle and its users.
