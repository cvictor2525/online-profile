Design for Insight: Compact SUV

長安歐尚 X5 2020 定位為緊湊型 SUV。內裝以橫向貫穿式儀錶板與貫穿式隱形出風口建立視覺秩序，運動包覆座椅與細分化儲物空間形成功能分區。中控配備 10 吋輕薄觸控屏，採多層級介面設計並可自訂主界面，整合無線充電與手機投屏功能。方向盤具盲操特性，操作按鍵提供觸感阻尼、燈光與聲音回饋。64 色氛圍燈與材質搭配（軟性覆層、金屬飾條）強化內裝的視覺節奏與觸感層次，確保功能整合、材質連續性與設計秩序在有限空間內取得平衡，呈現完整的內裝設計巧思。

The 2020 Changan Oshan X5, positioned as a compact SUV, features a horizontal wraparound dashboard and concealed air vents for visual order, complemented by sport-style seats and segmented storage. The 10‑inch thin touchscreen offers a multi-level, customizable interface, integrating wireless charging and phone projection. The steering wheel supports blind operation, and control buttons provide tactile, visual, and auditory feedback. A 64-color ambient lighting system, soft-touch surfaces, and metal trim reinforce material continuity, visual rhythm, and tactile quality, achieving balanced functional integration and structured interior logic within a compact space.

Design with a Clear Path/

設計源於連續性的探索，藉由訪談與行為觀察，將抽象一步步收斂成極具價值的邏輯架構，並在有限的資源與可實現性下，進行價值統整與排序，讓每一項設計決策皆可被追蹤，讓概念到具體形成清晰路徑。

Design emerges from continuous exploration. Through interviews and behavioral observation, abstract insights are progressively distilled into a coherent and high-value logical framework. Within constraints of resources and feasibility, value is synthesized and prioritized, ensuring that every design decision is traceable and that a clear path is formed from concept to execution.
