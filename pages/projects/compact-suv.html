Design for Insight: Compact SUV

長安歐尚 X5 2020 定位為緊湊型 SUV。內裝以橫向貫穿式儀錶板與貫穿式隱形出風口建立視覺秩序，運動包覆座椅與細分化儲物空間形成功能分區。中控配備 10 吋輕薄觸控屏，採多層級介面設計並可自訂主界面，整合無線充電與手機投屏功能。方向盤具盲操特性，操作按鍵提供觸感阻尼、燈光與聲音回饋。64 色氛圍燈與材質搭配（軟性覆層、金屬飾條）強化內裝的視覺節奏與觸感層次，確保功能整合、材質連續性與設計秩序在有限空間內取得平衡，呈現完整的內裝設計巧思。

The 2020 Changan Oshan X5, positioned as a compact SUV, features a horizontal wraparound dashboard and concealed air vents for visual order, complemented by sport-style seats and segmented storage. The 10‑inch thin touchscreen offers a multi-level, customizable interface, integrating wireless charging and phone projection. The steering wheel supports blind operation, and control buttons provide tactile, visual, and auditory feedback. A 64-color ambient lighting system, soft-touch surfaces, and metal trim reinforce material continuity, visual rhythm, and tactile quality, achieving balanced functional integration and structured interior logic within a compact space.

YEAR

2019

PROJECT

Changan OUSHAN X5

CLIENT

Changan Automobile

ROLE

Industrial Designer

APPROACH

In-depth Interview, Focus Group, User Workshop, Design Strategy, Digital Sketch

人物畫像/

此處由UX所主導，依據車主座談會、過往購車用戶、4S汽車店經理，共同歸化出兩大類用戶畫像，分別為初巢男主與後青春男孩。

In a UX-led initiative, we collaborated with car owners, historical buyers, and 4S store managers to develop two distinct personas: 'New-Nest Husbands' and 'Post-Youth Boys.'

用戶訪談與用戶旅程地圖/

參與UX團隊進行用戶訪談，解析用戶未用車時的狀況，如購買決策或用車前後等。其後搭乘用戶車輛，安排指定任務，透過脈絡訪查觀察用戶操作行為，以及放聲思考法了解用戶第一感受，並以場景切換最為顆粒度，最大程度地攤開用戶日常習慣。

Participated in user interviews to explore scenarios beyond driving, including buying decisions and vehicle preparation. Through ride-along studies with assigned tasks, I applied contextual inquiry and think-aloud methods to observe interactions and feelings. Focusing on context-switching as the unit of analysis, we aimed to maximize the visibility of the user's everyday habits.

痛點與期望/

透過一系列訪談，我們資料分成痛點和期望做排序，兩者皆依照以價值程度性和頻率高低綜合排序，能直觀的看出用戶的心理排序。

Through systematic interviews, we sorted pain points and expectations by their value impact and frequency. This dual-axis prioritization offers a clear and intuitive view of the user's internal ranking of needs.

設計工作坊/

設計團隊、潛在用戶與車主共同進行設計工作坊，我們先與汽車專家共同討論設計新趨勢，其後針對三大主題，包含造型、體驗與互動操作進行了多場景的意見交流，接著透過腦袋風暴、KJ法、故事版與世界咖啡等方式，以團隊分組方式創造多面性的設計切入點。

Our design workshops brought together experts, users, and owners. After identifying new design trends with automotive specialists, we explored aesthetic, experiential, and interactive possibilities across various scenarios. Through group sessions utilizing Brainstorming, KJ mapping, Storyboarding, and World Café, we uncovered a wide range of creative design directions.

Design with a Clear Path/

設計源於連續性的探索，藉由訪談與行為觀察，將抽象一步步收斂成極具價值的邏輯架構，並在有限的資源與可實現性下，進行價值統整與排序，讓每一項設計決策皆可被追蹤，讓概念到具體形成清晰路徑。

Design emerges from continuous exploration. Through interviews and behavioral observation, abstract insights are progressively distilled into a coherent and high-value logical framework. Within constraints of resources and feasibility, value is synthesized and prioritized, ensuring that every design decision is traceable and that a clear path is formed from concept to execution.
