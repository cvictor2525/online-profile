Designed for Adaptability: Floodlight Camera

Floodlight Camera 將居家安全完整融入美國家庭中。燈具本體具備多角度轉動，能安裝於狹道、轉角等多種使用場景；同時可外掛毫米波雷達等配件，提供大範圍精準偵測。產品重心與本體結構經過精密規劃，搭配簡約的外觀造型，使監控設備兼具功能性與美學設計，成為自然融入空間的居家設計元素。

Floodlight Camera fully integrates home security into the House in the US. The lamp body features multi-angle rotation, installing to various scenarios such as narrow corridors and corners. It can also be equipped with accessories like millimeter-wave radar for wide-range, precise detection. The product’s center of gravity and structural design are carefully engineered, paired with a minimalist exterior, allowing the device to combine functionality with aesthetic appeal and naturally blend into the home environment.

Unobtrusive Security/

過度的存在，意味著惱人的警訊；舒適的安全感是你幾乎感受不到它，卻在必要時刻即時出現。設計上，我們先覆蓋多種使用情境，再拆分為不同基礎單元，確保了耐久穩定性，也保留了一致性的視覺。

Excessive presence often reads as an intrusive alert, while comfortable security is something you barely notice, yet it responds immediately when needed. In terms of design, we first addressed a variety of usage scenarios, then broke the system into modular units to ensure durability and stability, while maintaining a consistent visual language.
