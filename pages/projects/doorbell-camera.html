Design for Small Spaces: Wired Video Doorbell Camera

Wired Video Doorbell Camera 以串聯室內與戶外空間為核心，結合 AI 辨識與 180° 超廣角鏡頭，協助使用者清楚辨識訪客、家庭成員與包裹，並透過 App 即時查看與通話，降低對門外狀況的不確定感；影像系統採用進階校正演算法以提升畫面清晰度，搭配 6000mAh 高容量電池，提供更高的安裝彈性與穩定的使用體驗。

Wired Video Doorbell Camera is designed to bridge indoor and outdoor spaces, integrating AI recognition with a 180° ultra-wide lens to help users clearly identify visitors, family members, and deliveries. Through the app, users can view and communicate in real time, reducing uncertainty about what’s happening outside the door; advanced image-correction algorithms enhance visual clarity, while a 6000mAh high-capacity battery provides greater installation flexibility and a stable, reliable user experience.

Finding the Balance:

如何在狹小的容器中實現多重的使用場景，該是犧牲還是價值排序？我們透過堆疊與型態的設計巧思找出一個良好的平衡點，實現優雅的互動體驗。

How can multiple user scenarios be realized within a compact enclosure—through compromise or prioritization? By experimenting with stacked component arrangements and form, we identified an optimal balance, achieving an elegant and coherent interactive experience.
