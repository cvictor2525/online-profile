Designed for Adaptability: Floodlight Camera

Floodlight Camera 將居家安全完整融入美國家庭中。燈具本體具備多角度轉動，能安裝於狹道、轉角等多種使用場景；同時可外掛毫米波雷達等配件，提供大範圍精準偵測。產品重心與本體結構經過精密規劃，搭配簡約的外觀造型，使監控設備兼具功能性與美學設計，成為自然融入空間的居家設計元素。

Floodlight Camera fully integrates home security into the House in the US. The lamp body features multi-angle rotation, installing to various scenarios such as narrow corridors and corners. It can also be equipped with accessories like millimeter-wave radar for wide-range, precise detection. The product’s center of gravity and structural design are carefully engineered, paired with a minimalist exterior, allowing the device to combine functionality with aesthetic appeal and naturally blend into the home environment.

YEAR

2020

PROJECT

Floodlight Camera

CLIENT

Private Label(U.S. market)

ROLE

Industrial Designer

APPROACH

Competitive Analysis, Ergonomics, Product Design, CMF

競品分析/

瞄準北美目標市場，分析亞馬遜購入熱門程度最高的前十名競品，進行規格比較與價格定位參考；同時根據評論進行量化統計，分析軟硬體的痛點作為日後可行性研究主題。

Target the North American market by analyzing the top ten best-selling products on Amazon, conducting specification comparisons and price positioning references. At the same time, perform quantitative analysis of customer reviews to identify hardware and software pain points, which will serve as key topics for future feasibility studies.

結構與場景研究/

根據不同產品進行安裝與結構分析，分辨不同產品的配重結構、接電方式、人體工學、使用場景與材質等進一步研究，並偕同競品分析之評論進行交叉結論。

Conduct installation and structural analysis across different products, systematically comparing weight distribution design, power supply methods, ergonomics, usage scenarios, and material characteristics. Integrate insights from competitive product evaluations to cross-validate findings and derive more comprehensive conclusions.

設計發展/

2D圖面設計發展，針對功能面探討其轉動角度、重心配置與場景覆蓋範圍，同時分析造型面包含外觀辨識度、造型語言與色彩運用，作為團隊快速評估與可行性討論的依據。

Develop 2D drawings to explore functional aspects such as rotation angles, center of gravity distribution, and scene coverage. At the same time, evaluate design aspects including visual identity, form language, and color application, serving as a basis for team discussion and rapid feasibility assessment.

Unobtrusive Security/

過度的存在，意味著惱人的警訊；舒適的安全感是你幾乎感受不到它，卻在必要時刻即時出現。設計上，我們先覆蓋多種使用情境，再拆分為不同基礎單元，確保了耐久穩定性，也保留了一致性的視覺。

Excessive presence often reads as an intrusive alert, while comfortable security is something you barely notice, yet it responds immediately when needed. In terms of design, we first addressed a variety of usage scenarios, then broke the system into modular units to ensure durability and stability, while maintaining a consistent visual language.
